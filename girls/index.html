Frequently Asked Questions

How long do the offers take? ⌄
Most users complete the required offers within about 5–7 days, but some offers can take longer depending on their terms (sometimes up to 60 days).
What are “offers”? ⌄
Partner offers can include mobile apps/games, trials, subscriptions, and services. Some are free, while many require a purchase or paid subscription to earn credit.
How many offers do I have to complete? ⌄
The number depends on the reward option and the program requirements shown on the next page. Each “level” is a group of offers with its own completion requirement.
When will I receive my reward? ⌄
After completing the required offers, you’ll go through the claim process. Verification and delivery can take a few business days. Many users receive delivery within about 6–10 days of registration (varies by offer timing and verification).
